About

I am a researcher focusing on transportation systems, mathematical optimization, and shared autonomous vehicle (SAV) operations. My work explores flexible route planning, depot siting, charging infrastructure, and multimodal integration with a particular interest in Benders decomposition and scalable algorithms for large space–time networks.

I am completing my Ph.D. at the University of Tsukuba (Social Engineering). I received my B.Eng. in Logistics Engineering from Shanghai Ocean University.

Research Interests

  • Shared autonomous vehicles (SAV) operation & infrastructure design
  • Carpooling & ridesharing optimization; fairness and pricing
  • Space–time expanded networks; traffic assignment with demand-side behavior
  • Decomposition methods (Benders), heuristics, and MIP modeling (Gurobi)
